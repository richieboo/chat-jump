SMS Contact Filter

Stay focused in Google Messages™. Instantly surface the conversation you need with blazing-fast filtering, bulk search options, and helpers tailored for power users.

Highlights

  • Filter contacts and threads in real time as you type.
  • Open matching conversations directly without leaving the sidebar.
  • Run deep-dive scans that step through every chat automatically when you need to search message contents.
  • Hide distractions and jump straight to the people you chat with most.

Getting the Most Out of It

  • Use the toggle button to keep the interface clean when you are done.
  • Press Enter to run a full-content search, or Esc to reset.
  • Hover the match list to preview conversations before jumping in.
  • Need help? Reach out via the project repository or the Chrome Web Store listing.
